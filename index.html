Welcome

It is with great pleasure we welcome you to the 14th meeting of the International Skeletal Dysplasia Society (ISDS). The meeting will be held in Oslo, capital of Norway, from the 11th to the 14th of September 2019. This is the first time the ISDS meeting will be held in Scandinavia. In tradition with the previous meetings we will do our utmost to offer you a top scientific program and an enjoyable stay.

Hope to see you all in Oslo.

Yours sincerely Else Merckoll and Cecilie F. Rustad
